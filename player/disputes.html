How disputes & complaints work
Dispute a match. Open within 48 h of result submission. Admin reviews both submissions, both pieces of evidence, and decides: A wins / B wins / cancelled / replay / mark frivolous (5 % fee on disputer).
File a complaint on a held prize. Only available during the 48 h hold and only to other participants in the same competition. Freezes the prize for admin review.
Frivolous threshold. Three frivolous-dispute strikes in a rolling 12 mo triggers a 30 d host-suspension.
