§ 01

Getting started

5 questions

Who Boadman is for, what you can play, what verification looks like.

What is Boadman?

Boadman is a competition platform for players in supported regions. You enter 1v1 challenges and single-match tournaments against other players, with prize pools paid in coins — our native virtual currency, GBP-pegged at 1 coin = £0.10. It is not gambling: outcomes depend on player skill, not chance. The platform takes a commission on every settled match.

See: How it worksTerms of Service § 1
Is it free to sign up?

Yes. Account creation costs nothing and you start with 100 demo coins that can only be used in challenge-mode practice matches against the platform bot or other demo-mode players. Demo coins cannot be cashed out and have no GBP value. To play for real coins you need to deposit GBP via Stripe.

What games are supported at launch?

Apex Legends, Cyberpunk 2077, FC25, Valorant and Call of Duty. More titles roll out monthly based on player demand and Publisher verification. If you want a specific game added, drop a vote at support@boadman.com.

5 games live · v1.0 · 2026-07
How does identity verification work?

We use a tiered KYC model. L1 (government ID + selfie via Didit) is triggered when your rolling-30-day deposits exceed £200, or before your first cash-out. L2 (enhanced Didit IDV with proof of address + source-of-funds attestation) kicks in for single cash-outs above £2,000, or rolling-12-month cash-outs above £5,000. Most decisions are automatic in under five minutes.

Required by applicable AML regulationProvider: Didit
Which countries can play?

Supported regions only at launch. Boadman is AML-registered in the regions where it operates. Other regions are on the roadmap but not yet available. VPN bypass is a Terms of Service violation and results in account suspension and forfeiture of any pending withdrawals.

Supported regions onlyGeo-block: North Korea, Iran, Myanmar

Coins, deposits & cash-outs

6 questions

How the closed-loop coin economy works — buying, spending, withdrawing.

What is a Boadman coin?

A coin is Boadman's native virtual token — distinct from pence, not a re-brand. The platform pegs 1 coin to £0.10 (10 pence) by configuration. Coins are not a cryptocurrency, have no blockchain, and cannot be transferred between players outside of competition escrow. They only redeem to the original payment source under closed-loop redemption.

Rate: 1 coin = £0.10Pending SP8-ECON economics review
How do I buy coins?

From /wallet → Deposit. Pay in GBP via Stripe (card or Apple/Google Pay in the UK) or NGN via Paystack (Nigeria). Coins are credited to your spendable balance instantly when the payment provider confirms the charge via webhook — typically under 10 seconds. Failed or abandoned purchases do not credit any coins.

Min deposit: £5Max single deposit: £1,000
How long does a cash-out take?

Bank transfer: 1–3 UK business days once dispatched (Stripe Financial Connections verified bank in your legal name). Card refund (within 180 days of original deposit): same business day, back to the original card. Cash-outs above £2,000 in any 7-day window trigger an MLRO review with a 24-hour target before dispatch.

Closed-loop redemption onlyMin cash-out: 100 coins (£10)
What's the cash-out fee?Provisional

A combined 13% on cash-outs — 10% platform fee plus 3% to cover Stripe and FX rails. Subject to a floor of 20 coins minimum so micro-withdrawals don't bleed the platform. The exact split between platform and rails is under review.

Worked example: cash out 1,000 coins → receive £87.00
Why was my cash-out frozen?

Your wallet was flagged for AML review under applicable Anti-Money-Laundering regulation. Triggers include unusual velocity (e.g. depositing > £2k in 24h), redeeming shortly after a large deposit, or a sanctions-list near-match. Your coin balance is preserved — nothing is lost. Our MLRO targets resolution within 72 hours; you'll receive an email with the outcome and any remediation steps.

Independent of account status
Can I close my account?

Yes. Go to /profile → Data rights → Delete account. Your personal data is anonymised immediately, your profile becomes inaccessible, and any remaining coin balance is cashed out to your verified payout method (subject to KYC). Per applicable AML retention (5-year minimum), financial, KYC and AML records are retained for 5 years in anonymised form before final erasure — a non-negotiable regulatory minimum.

Playing challenges & tournaments

6 questions

Match formats, escrow, disputes, prize holds, hosting your own.

How does a 1v1 challenge work?Provisional

Pick an opponent, choose a wager (100–10,000 coins) and a format (single match or best-of-3), and send the invite. Both stakes are locked into platform escrow on accept. Play in-game, then both submit results with optional evidence (clip URL or screenshot). On agreement the winner receives the pot net of 12% commission; on disagreement it goes to dispute.

Rates illustrative — pending SP8-ECON economics review.

See: Terms § 4
What if my opponent and I disagree on the result?

Either player can file a dispute within 48 hours of result submission. Attach evidence — clip URL, stream link, or screenshot. The match prize is frozen and routed to our admin dispute queue with a 72-hour SLA. Outcomes: A wins, B wins, cancelled (both refunded), replay, or frivolous — which deducts a 5% fee from the disputing player and adds a strike. Three strikes in 12 months equals a 30-day host-suspension.

Evidence purged 60 days post-match (GDPR)
How are tournaments different from 1v1s?Provisional

Tournaments are multi-player single-match brackets — typically 8 to 32 entrants. The default prize split is 60% / 30% / 10% to the top three. Player-hosted tournaments cost a 2-coin host fee and the platform takes 12% commission. Brand-hosted tournaments take 15% commission with no host fee. Hosts release the prize once the bracket completes.

Rates illustrative — pending SP8-ECON economics review.

Formats: single-elim, double-elim, round-robin
Can I host my own tournament?

Yes. Go to /tournaments/new, choose game, format, entry fee, and bracket size. You'll pay a 2-coin host fee on creation. KYC L1 is required to host — you cannot host on an unverified account. As host you're responsible for releasing the prize within 7 days of the scheduled end; failing to do so loses the host fee and counts as a strike.

Requires KYC L1Min bracket: 4 players
What happens to coins if a tournament is cancelled?

If the host cancels before the bracket starts, all entry fees are refunded in full to participants — but the 2-coin host fee is non-refundable (it discourages frivolous tournament creation). If an admin force-cancels mid-bracket due to host abandonment or an MLRO action, refunds are pro-rated based on progress, with a public audit-log entry explaining the decision.

Why is my prize on a 48-hour hold?

It's the standard complaint window. Every prize from a 1v1 challenge or player/brand-hosted tournament sits in a 48-hour hold so another participant in the same competition can flag a cheating or rule-breach concern. After 48 hours with no complaint, the prize matures automatically and the coins are spendable. A filed complaint freezes the prize for admin review.

Admin-hosted tournaments skip the hold

Safety, fairness & responsible play

5 questions

Regulation, anti-cheat, deposit limits, data, account security.

Is Boadman gambling?

No. Boadman is skill-based competition wagering between consenting peers. Match outcomes depend on player skill, not chance, which places us outside gambling regulators' regulatory scope (skill-only contests are typically carved out). We operate under applicable Anti-Money-Laundering regulation as a financial-services platform, AML-registered for supervision.

Not a gambling licenceAML-registered
How do you stop cheating?

Both players submit results with optional evidence. Automated checks compare timestamp consistency, repeat-opponent patterns, account-age signals, win-rate anomalies, and known stream metadata. Suspicious matches are auto-flagged to the MLRO for review. Confirmed cheating results in match reversal, prize forfeit, and account suspension or ban. Public leaderboards are recalculated on every reversal.

Can I get help if I'm spending too much?

Yes. Set daily and weekly deposit limits in /profile → Communication & limits — changes that decrease a limit apply immediately; increases are delayed by 24 hours by design. Self-exclusion (a hard freeze on deposits and entries for a chosen window) is contact-support-only in v1; email support@boadman.com and we'll action it within one business day.

Find a local helpline at your region's public-health resources
Is my data sold or shared with advertisers?

No. We never sell personal data. Consent at sign-up is granular: analytics is opt-in (defaults off), marketing emails are explicit-opt-in only, and we never share match history or wallet data with third parties for advertising. We share with regulators when legally required (e.g. Suspicious Activity Reports). Full detail at /legal/privacy.

applicable data-protection law
What if I lose my password?

Go to /signin → Forgot password and enter the email on your account. You'll receive a single-use reset link valid for 60 minutes. Passwords are hashed — no Boadman staff can ever see or recover your password, only force a reset. If you've also lost access to your email, contact support@boadman.com with identity evidence and we'll manually verify before unlocking.

Hashing: bcrypt via Supabase Auth

Brand & sponsorship

4 questions

For companies that want to host tournaments or fund prize pools.

Can my brand sponsor a tournament?Provisional

Yes. Register your company at /signup-brand with its Companies House number (UK) or CAC number (NG). After approval — typically 1–2 business days — fund your brand wallet via Stripe and contribute to any open tournament via the sponsorship flow. Brand-hosted tournaments take 15% commission (vs 12% player-hosted). Sponsorship contributions are publicly attributed on the tournament page.

Rates illustrative — pending SP8-ECON economics review.

Can my brand wallet cash out?

No. Brand wallets are spend-only by design — this is core to our closed-loop coin economy and a key part of why we're not a money-services business. You can fund the wallet, host tournaments, sponsor prize pools, and pay the platform commission, but the balance never returns to GBP. Plan brand wallet top-ups against a concrete sponsorship calendar.

closed-loop compliance
Where does my brand's logo appear?

On every tournament you host or sponsor: the tournament detail page, match cards in the live bracket, the public tournament-finder card, and the post-match results screen. Premium-tier sponsorships also get logo placement on the live-readout HUD during the match. A public sponsorships list shows every contributor and the amount contributed (in coins).

Can multiple managers share access to one brand?

Yes — though v1 ships single-owner only. The brand application submitter becomes the initial owner. Additional managers (full operational access, no ownership transfer) can be invited from the brand dashboard once the multi-manager UI lands in SP24. Until then, contact support@boadman.com for manual manager addition with written owner consent.

Roles: owner · managerMulti-manager UI: SP24
