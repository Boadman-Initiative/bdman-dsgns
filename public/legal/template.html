Scope & acceptance

By creating an account, depositing coins, joining competitions or otherwise using the platform you agree to these Terms, to our Privacy Policy and to our Cookie Policy. If you don't accept, you must not use the platform.

Important · applicable AML regulation Boadman operates under applicable Anti-Money-Laundering regulation as a financial-services platform. Identity verification, record-keeping and reporting obligations may affect how you deposit and cash out.

Eligibility & identity verification

You must be 18 years or older and resident in a country we support (currently in supported regions) to register and use the platform. Persons resident in countries on our geographic blacklist (currently North Korea, Iran and Myanmar) may not sign up or sign in.

Identity verification (KYC) is mandatory for any redemption and is also triggered automatically when cumulative deposits in any rolling 30-day window exceed £200. Enhanced verification (L2) is required for any single redemption above £2,000 or rolling 12-month redemption total above £5,000.

Your Boadman wallet

Boadman uses a native virtual unit called the coin. The coin is not a re-brand of pence. The conversion rate is set in platform configuration; at the date these Terms take effect the rate is 1 coin = 10 pence (£0.10). Rates are subject to change on notice.

Funds deposited via Stripe (UK / EU) or Paystack (Nigeria) credit your wallet as coins. Redemptions return coins to fiat and are paid only to a destination verified to belong to you in your declared legal name (closed-loop redemption).

Competitions & prizes

Boadman supports 1v1 challenges, single-match free-for-all tournaments and brand-sponsored competitions. Prizes are paid in coins. After release, every prize sits in a 48-hour hold during which any participant in the same competition may file a complaint; the hold then matures automatically.

Disputes & complaints

You may dispute a match within 48 hours of result submission. Our admin team reviews submissions, evidence and history. Outcomes include: participant A wins, participant B wins, cancelled (both refunded), replay (results wiped, match re-activated) or frivolous-dispute (5% fee deducted from the disputing player).

AML, sanctions & suspensions

Activity that triggers our anti-money-laundering rules may result in a cash-out freeze, an account suspension, or, in extremis, a permanent ban. Suspensions and bans are recorded in our audit log and may be reported to the National Crime Agency under our Suspicious Activity Report obligations.

Data & privacy

Our handling of personal data is governed by the Privacy Policy. Notable retention: financial, KYC and AML records are kept for 5 years after closure, as required by applicable AML retention (5-year minimum), even where a profile has been anonymised.

Fees & commissions

Provisional rates (interim, subject to economics review):

  • Challenge commission: 12% of pot
  • Tournament commission: 12% (player-hosted) / 15% (brand-hosted)
  • Publisher royalty: 15% of platform commission
  • Redemption fee: greater of (a) 20-coin minimum, or (b) 10 coins + 3% of redemption amount
  • Player-hosted tournament host fee: 2 coins
  • Cancellation fee on a post-accept 1v1: 2% of stake
  • Frivolous-dispute fee: 5% of stake
  • Abandoned-match fee: 5% of stake (each player)
