Logo & lockup
exploration

The Spartan helmet is the brand. Six lockup explorations below — pick the one (or two — display + monogram) that the team takes forward into production. Each carries its own use-case and tone, so the system depends less on a single hero treatment and more on contextual fit.

A

Full horizontal lockup

The everyday signature — used in the app header, the marketing site header, on physical media, and as the e-mail header. Helmet mark is flush-left of the wordmark; "BOADMAN" rendered in Barlow Condensed 800 with 0.12em tracking — military stamp, not corporate sans.

Recommendation

Pick a primary + a mark-only and the system is complete. My recommendation:

BOADMAN
Approved set · A2 + B1 + C1 + D3/D4 + E1

A2 (helmet + clean Barlow Condensed wordmark) — primary in-product and marketing surfaces.
B1 (tactical badge, neutral slate) — sidebar mark when wordmark won't fit.
C1 (vertical lockup with tagline) — posters, prize PDFs, presentation decks.
D3 / D4 (notification dark · print paper) — push notifications and print materials.
E1 (splash motion) — first-load and sign-in surface.

B3 dropped — flame-fill clashed with the brand-orange background of the in-product UI. We'll commission a dedicated app-icon mark separately so the home-screen face doesn't compete with the in-product accent.
